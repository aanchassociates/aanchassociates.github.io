Document Collection on WhatsApp

We send a precise checklist for your specific service. You share documents on WhatsApp. We verify every document for completeness before filing — flagging any issues immediately so there are no delays later.

What we handle

📊 Throughout

Proactive Status Updates

You never need to ask "what is happening?" We send updates at every milestone — Part A submitted, ARN received, under review, query raised, approved. If a government portal raises a query, we respond within 24 hours.

Update milestones

  • Application submitted confirmation
  • Acknowledgement / ARN number shared
  • Any officer queries handled proactively
  • Approval notification immediately
  • Expected delivery date always communicated

Your certificate is delivered digitally with a clear explanation of what it means, what comes next, and any ongoing compliance obligations. Balance payment via UPI on delivery. We then advise on a monthly retainer if ongoing compliance applies.

Delivery includes

  • 30-day post-delivery query support
